About PurrfectMind

Our story, mission, and the technology behind our mental wellness chatbot

Our Story

PurrfectMind began in 2022 when our founder, Dr. Samantha Chen, recognized a critical gap in mental health support: accessibility. As a clinical psychologist with over 15 years of experience, she saw firsthand how many people struggled to access timely support when they needed it most.

After losing a close friend to suicide, Dr. Chen became determined to create a solution that could provide immediate support to anyone, anywhere, at any time. She assembled a team of mental health professionals, AI specialists, and compassionate designers to build PurrfectMind.

Today, PurrfectMind serves over 500,000 users worldwide, providing a judgment-free space for people to express their feelings, learn coping strategies, and bridge the gap to professional help when needed.

Our Mission

Making mental wellness support accessible to everyone, everywhere

Accessibility

We believe mental health support should be available to everyone, regardless of location, time, or financial means.

Education

We aim to help people understand their emotions and learn evidence-based techniques for managing their mental health.

Bridge to Professional Care

We complement, not replace, professional mental healthcare by supporting users in seeking appropriate help when needed.
